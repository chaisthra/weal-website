Health Tips

Make sure to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day

Incorporate physical activity into your daily routine, such as taking a walk or doing some light exercise

Eat a balanced and nutritious diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein

Get enough sleep each night to allow your body to rest and rejuvenate

Practice stress-reducing techniques like yoga or meditation to promote mental wellness

Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption to maintain good overall health

Stay socially connected with friends and loved ones, as social support can have positive effects on mental and physical health

General Physician
Dr. G. Ramaiah

A true veteran of the medical profession, Dr. G. Ramaiah has dedicated his life to providing compassionate and expert care to patients. Having entered state government services as an LMP doctor in 1958, Dr. Ramaiah went on to pass his MBBS examination in 1965, serving in various roles within the Karnataka state government services until his retirement in 1994. Even after retiring from his government role, he has continued to provide medical services through his private practice, drawing on his extensive experience and knowledge to deliver exceptional care to his patients with over 60 years of experience
